Gay Chicago

Speaking of gay Chicago we can not ignore its vibrant gay scene and its fastest growing gay populations in the country. Chicago is famous for its Boystown, the world’s most “Incomparable Gay Neighborhood”. Boystown Chicago is situated just southeast of Wrigleyville in Lakeview, it is the first officially recognized gay village in the United States.

Chicago has a vibrant gay bar scene, but not all of gay bars, clubs are in Boystown. You will find some nice and cool gay bars scattered in Chicago. If you are not a Boystown fan, then places like uptown, Lake View and Rogers Park provides some gay bars that are as good as those in Boystown.

Gay Bars in Chicago

We picked some gay bars in Chicago that offer something for every taste.

Roscoe’s Tavern

Opened on April 1,1987 it helped to change the face of the surrounding neighborhood and the face of the “gay bar” business in Chicago. Roscoe’s continues to flourish today, and it is a fun and entertaining place for the most adventuresome customer.

Progress Bar

The hottest new gay bar in Boystown staffed by some very familiar faces. DESIGNED TO BE LIKE NOTHING YOU’VE EVER SEEN BEFORE! If you visit Boystown Chicago, do not miss this gay bar.
