Libertarian Position on Gay Marriage

We welcome anyone who believes in individual liberty and who wishes to actively promote gay rights. Believing in minimum government, maximum Freedom. The critical spirit never knows when to stop meddling. -- Mason Cooley

We works to improve the lives of gay men worldwide by advocating for equal rights of gay marriage. Gay marriage should have access to all the benefits enjoyed by different-sex couples. Gay marriage is protected by the US Constitution's commitments to liberty and equality. Denying some people the option to marry is discriminatory.
